Breaking the surface
30th September, 2014

Hello world. This is where we start. 30th September, 2014. The date Sinthetic Labs was born. So what is Sinthetic Labs, who are we, and why are we here?

Sinthetic Labs is a security research group which aims to make the web a safer place by taking an offensive approach to security. You can read more about what we do on our homepage. But who are we? My name is Nathan Malcolm. I'm a security researcher, penetration tester, developer, freedom lover, and I have a beard. I live in the United Kingdom -- home of Lizzy and Charles -- where I spend most of my time at my desk hacking away. Just me, for now, but I hope I can find other guys with beards to work with too. It's a little too soon to divulge what we're working on but we hope it will be as useful to the community as it is to us.

Stay tuned for lots of goodies.
